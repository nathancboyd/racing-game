Racing Game

*CONTROLS*

W - Forward
S - Reverse

Left Arrow - Turn left
Right Arrow - Turn right

A - Strafe Left
D - Strafe Right

Shift - Boost
Space - Airbrake
Q - Rear Camera
E - Fire
R - Reset Lap

B - Jump (this is cheating)

Up Arrow - Switch to high thrust, high drag ship
Down Arrow - Switch to low thrust, low drag ship
